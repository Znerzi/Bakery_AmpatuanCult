Our History

Simultaneously with their academic pursuits, the group, spearheaded by Carl Boquiron, Jhodel Datiles, and six other members, embarked on the journey of starting a business while still immersed in their studies. Recognizing the potential synergy between their venture and their Bachelor of Science in Information Technology course, they strategically incorporated their business plan with the prevailing technological trends of the current year. Opting for the name 7-Evelyn Bakery, they set their sights on leveraging emerging technologies to establish their brand. Their primary goal is to offer affordable products accessible to the masses, and they emphasize that the significance of their endeavor should not be underestimated. The collective vision is not just about creating a bakery; it's a commitment to merging technology, education, and affordability in a venture that resonates with the evolving needs of the community. 7evelyn Bakery
